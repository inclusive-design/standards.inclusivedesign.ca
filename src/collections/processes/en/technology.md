## Summary

Technology involves using tools that are accessible and familiar to all participants. Accessible technology works with assistive tools, is easy to use, and includes support when challenges or technical issues arise.

## Why is this important?

Technology plays an important role in making standards development more inclusive because the right tools make it easier for everyone to contribute. Choosing accessible technology helps prevent people from being unintentionally excluded because of barriers with digital tools.
